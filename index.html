About

We are a small group of enthusiastic physicists, computer scientists, and electrical engineers based in Stuttgart, Germany, with strong ties to the University of Stuttgart.

Our goal is to provide high-quality electronics and service for research and development at a highly competitive price.

Our focus is on detection and generation of fast arbitrary digital signals.

Currently two of our products are available: TimeTagger 20 and PulseStreamer 8/2
