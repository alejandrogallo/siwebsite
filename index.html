About

We are a small group of enthusiastic physicists, computer scientists, and electrical engineers based in Stuttgart, Germany, with strong ties to the University of Stuttgart.

Our corporate goal is to provide high-quality electronics and service for our colleagues in the education, research, and development fields at highly competitive prices.

Our focus is on detection and generation of reliable, fast arbitrary digital signals and our long term interest is to create a range of interoperable instruments with superior characteristics and supporting complex control-loop feedback experiments.

Currently two of our products, TimeTagger and PulseStreamer, are commercially available, and two additional products, PulseEmitter and DigiLoop, are becoming available in 2016.

TimeTagger is an inexpensive, programmable, general-purpose, continuous-operation, high-speed, multi-channel digital signal acquisition device. The device is implemented on the basis of a Time-Tag architecture and views measurement events, such as laser-pulses and photon-emissions, as a continuous data-stream, consisting of an infinite number of events.

PulseStreamer is a streaming pulse generator capable of generating infinite streams of pulses, including white noise. It is the logical complement to TimeTagger and is currently available to the public as a Beta product. As a Beta product, PulseStreamer is priced of half of the device's planned retail price and comes with an extended Beta warranty, which guarantees that the Beta device will be updated to - or replaced with - a full release version once PulseStreamer goes into general release.

PulseEmitter is a programmable pulsating laser that can be be programmed using a USB connection and can be controlled by a pulse generator such as PulseStreamer 8/2. The device is capable of replacing acousto-optic modulators (AOM) and has terrific characteristics (it has a rise time that is quite a bit faster than an AOM, essentially infinite on/off ratio, and negligible delay, negating the need for programmed delays.) PulseEmitter is available as an Alpha product on very favorable terms. Contact us for details.

DigiLoop is a digital PID controller. This product is currently in the design phase and is not yet available to the public.
